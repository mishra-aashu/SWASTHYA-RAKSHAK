<div align="center"><h1>SWASTHYA RAKSHAK</h1><h3>(स्वास्थ्य रक्षक)</h3><p><strong>National Real-Time Health Surveillance System</strong></p><br /><p>Swasthya Rakshak is a comprehensive digital health monitoring platform designed to bridge the gap between Primary Health Centers (Swasth Kendras) and citizens. It creates a unified ecosystem for real-time disease tracking, predictive health analytics, and location-based safety alerts.</p><p><a href="https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/yourusername/swasthya-rakshak"><img src="https://www.google.com/search?q=https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Prototype-orange%3Fstyle%3Dfor-the-badge" alt="Status" /></a><a href="LICENSE"><img src="https://www.google.com/search?q=https://img.shields.io/badge/Stack-React%2520|%20Supabase-green?style=for-the-badge" alt="Tech Stack" /></a></p></div><hr /><h2>The Problem</h2><p>India faces significant challenges in disease surveillance:</p><ul><li><strong>Delayed Detection:</strong> Disease outbreaks often take 7-14 days to be identified centrally.</li><li><strong>Information Gap:</strong> Citizens lack real-time data about health risks in their specific neighborhoods.</li><li><strong>Siloed Data:</strong> Thousands of Primary Health Centers (PHCs) operate without a unified, instant reporting mechanism.</li></ul><hr /><h2>The Solution</h2><p>Swasthya Rakshak solves this by connecting three key stakeholders in a unified loop:</p><ol><li><strong>Citizens:</strong> Receive instant alerts and health guidance.</li><li><strong>Health Workers (Kendras):</strong> Use a simplified interface for rapid data entry.</li><li><strong>Government:</strong> Access a command center for national monitoring and resource allocation.</li></ol><hr /><h2>Key Features</h2><h3>For Citizens (User App)</h3><table><thead><tr><th>Feature</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Location Intelligence</strong></td><td>View a "Health Score" for your sector/district based on real-time data.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Instant Alerts</strong></td><td>Receive notifications for local outbreaks (e.g., "Dengue cases rising in Sector 62").</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Doctor Shorts</strong></td><td>Watch 60-second verified video tips from medical experts.</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Nearby Services</strong></td><td>Locate the nearest functional Swasth Kendra and check medicine availability.</td></tr></tbody></table><h3>For Health Workers (Kendra Dashboard)</h3><ul><li><strong>Rapid Data Entry:</strong> Log disease cases and patient stats in under 60 seconds.</li><li><strong>Inventory Management:</strong> Track medicine stock and receive low-stock alerts.</li><li><strong>Digital Reporting:</strong> Eliminate manual paperwork with instant syncing to the national database.</li></ul><h3>For Administration (Command Center)</h3><ul><li><strong>Heatmaps:</strong> Visualize disease spread across districts and states in real-time.</li><li><strong>AI Predictions:</strong> Forecast potential outbreaks based on historical data and weather patterns.</li><li><strong>Resource Allocation:</strong> Identify critical zones requiring immediate medical supplies or personnel.</li><li><strong>Disaster Response:</strong> Specialized modules for flood and natural disaster health management.</li></ul><hr /><h2>How It Works</h2><ol><li><strong>Data Collection:</strong> Health workers at Swasth Kendras input daily case data and medicine stock via the web portal.</li><li><strong>Processing:</strong> The system aggregates this data and correlates it with weather/geographic data.</li><li><strong>Analysis:</strong> The engine identifies trends, spikes, and potential outbreaks.</li><li><strong>Dissemination:</strong><ul><li>Citizens get alerts on their phones.</li><li>Admins see high-risk zones on the dashboard.</li><li>Doctors upload relevant guidance videos.</li></ul></li></ol><hr /><h2>Tech Stack</h2><table><thead><tr><th>Component</th><th>Technology</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>Frontend</strong></td><td>React.js, Vite, Tailwind CSS</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Backend</strong></td><td>Supabase (PostgreSQL)</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Maps</strong></td><td>Leaflet</td></tr><tr><td><strong>Charts</strong></td><td>Chart.js, Recharts</td></tr></tbody></table><hr /><h2>Getting Started</h2><p>Follow these steps to set up the project locally.</p><h3>Prerequisites</h3><ul><li>Node.js (v16 or higher)</li><li>npm or
